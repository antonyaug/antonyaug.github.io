About Me

I’m an enthusiastic engineer passionate about electric mobility, renewable energy, and automation. My projects focus on onboard chargers, battery pack design, and power electronics systems that contribute to sustainable transportation.

Projects

Onboard Charger for Plug-in EVs

Designed using an interleaved boost PFC and PSFB DC-DC converter to make EVs compatible with charging stations.

Battery Pack for Houseboat

Developed a solar-integrated Li-ion pack to reduce diesel generator usage on Kerala houseboats.

ICE Vehicle to EV Conversion Design - Volkswagen Polo GT

Designed a 50 kWh, 400 V NMC battery pack (108S25P, 2700 cells) with approx 280 km range, selected and integrated BorgWarner HVH 250 PMSM motor (150 kW, 320 Nm, ~96% efficiency) for high performance and reliability.
